THE SWAMP

Welcome to FrogTown — a living frog civilization and the center of everything

FrogMog isn't one game with a lobby. It's a platform — a frog civilization that frogs come home to between battles, races, brawls, and complete nonsense. Every mode is its own flavor of chaos. Some are dead serious. Some are completely unhinged. All of them are unmistakably frog.

FrogTown is the hub world — a 200×200 meter civilization built in the roots and branches of a colossal ancient tree. Named NPCs wander the market, fish by the pond, and mutter about secrets behind the waterfall. Between matches, this is home.

Every frog has 3 HP. You're fragile. You're fast. You have a tongue. Make it count.

FAQ

Everything you need to know about FrogMog

What is FrogMog?

FrogMog is a cross-platform multiplayer party game where up to 100 frogs battle, race, and brawl across an ever-growing collection of game modes. Drop into FrogTown — a living frog civilization — then jump into battle royale, kart racing, team brawls, and complete nonsense. Coming to iOS, Android, and Steam with cross-play day one.

What game modes does FrogMog have?

FrogMog launches with two confirmed modes and a massive pipeline:

  • FrogRoyale — 100-player gun-based battle royale with a shrinking zone
  • FrogDriver — Kart racing with lily pad power-ups and tongue-grapple shortcuts

The mode pipeline includes Tongue Tag, Fly Catcher, Lily Leap, Giant Frog, Capture The Fly, Pond Wars, Sniper Swamp, Frog Golf, ranked competitive modes, and more. New modes drop constantly — if it plays fun, it stays.

What platforms will FrogMog be on?

FrogMog is coming to iOS, Android, and Steam (PC, Mac, Linux) with cross-play from day one. VR support for Meta Quest is also on the roadmap — you'll actually play as the frog in first person.

How does combat work?

Every frog has 3 HP and three ways to deal damage:

  • Jump & Eat — Land on a frog from above or behind for an instant kill. The signature move.
  • Tongue Lash — Ranged cone attack with auto-aim. Deals 1 HP damage.
  • Stomp — Bounce off a frog mid-air. Deals 2 HP damage. Your panic move when the eat fails.
Can I customize my frog?

Absolutely. FrogMog has a deep cosmetic system with 20+ hats (crown, wizard hat, viking horns, bunny ears, mushroom cap, and growing), 10+ trails (sparkles, fire, rainbow, bubbles), full frog skins and colors, animated emotes, and mode-specific gear. Your frog should look like no other frog. New cosmetics drop fast — events, competitive rewards, streamer drops, and more.

Does FrogMog have streamer integration?

Yes — and it's a core pillar, not an afterthought. Twitch chat controls the chaos with commands like !rain (rain flies from the sky), !giant (make a random frog huge), !yeet (launch a frog into the sky), !backwards (reverse everyone's controls), and !chaos (everything at once). Chat doesn't watch — chat IS part of the game.

When does FrogMog come out?

FrogMog is targeting a public alpha in 2026. Sign up above to get notified when playable builds are ready, or join the Discord for real-time dev updates.

Is FrogMog free to play?

Pricing hasn't been announced yet. Our philosophy is fun first, money please — games should get more fun over time, not more monetized. Follow the Discord or sign up for email updates to be the first to know.

Who is TJ-Dev-Studio?

TJ-Dev-Studio is an indie game studio — two people who can't stop building. We started with frogs because frogs are funny. We kept going because the world wouldn't stop growing. You can support development through GitHub Sponsors.
