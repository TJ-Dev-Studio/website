DEV LOG

Welcome to FrogMog

TJ
TJ-Dev-Studio
March 5, 2026

We're ready to share FrogMog — a 3D multiplayer battle royale where up to 100 frogs duke it out in an ever-shrinking arena. This has been a passion project for us, and it's time to let the swamp out.

The Concept

100 frogs enter. One frog leaves. The arena starts as FrogTown — a living 200×200 meter world built in the roots of an ancient Mother Tree. NPCs wander the market stalls, fish at the pond, meditate behind a hidden waterfall. Then the match starts, and everything turns into chaos.

The Tech

FrogMog is built on Godot 4.4.1 (pure GDScript) with a Nakama authoritative game server running at 30 ticks per second. The entire world is declared in JSON — not a single node was hand-placed in an editor. 551 automated tests prove it works.

Platforms

FrogMog launches on iOS and Android, mobile-first. VR mode for Meta Quest 2 is on the roadmap — you'll actually be the frog.

What's Next

We're deep in development, locking down core movement, the battle royale loop, and the authoritative server. Sign up below to get notified when playable builds are ready. We'd love to have you in the swamp.

FAQ

Everything you need to know about FrogMog

What is FrogMog?

FrogMog is a chaotic 3D multiplayer battle royale game where up to 100 frogs fight in a shrinking arena called FrogTown. Each frog has 3 HP and three attack types: Jump & Eat (instant kill), Tongue Lash (1 HP ranged attack), and Stomp (2 HP mid-air bounce). Built with Godot 4.4.1 and Nakama authoritative server, FrogMog is coming to iOS and Android.

How many players can play FrogMog at once?

FrogMog supports up to 100 players in a single match. All frogs drop into FrogTown simultaneously, and the zone shrinks until one frog remains. The Nakama server runs at 30 ticks per second to keep gameplay smooth at scale.

What platforms will FrogMog be on?

FrogMog is being built mobile-first for iOS and Android. VR support for Meta Quest 2 is on the roadmap — you'll actually play as the frog in first person.

What game engine is FrogMog built with?

FrogMog is built with Godot 4.4.1 using pure GDScript, with a Nakama authoritative game server. The entire world is declared in JSON — zero nodes hand-placed in the editor. Over 551 automated tests validate the codebase.

How does combat work in FrogMog?

Every frog has 3 HP and three ways to deal damage:

  • Jump & Eat — Land on a frog from above or behind for an instant kill. The signature move.
  • Tongue Lash — Ranged cone attack with auto-aim. Deals 1 HP damage.
  • Stomp — Bounce off a frog mid-air. Deals 2 HP damage. Your panic move when the eat fails.
When does FrogMog come out?

FrogMog is in active development. The team is locking down core movement, the battle royale loop, and authoritative server networking. Sign up above to get notified when playable builds are ready.

Is FrogMog free to play?

Pricing hasn't been announced yet. Follow the Discord or sign up for email updates to be the first to know.

Who is TJ-Dev-Studio?
